What We Are!

Kaveri GS AutoMech is an ISO 9001:2015 certified precision machining company based in Chakan, Pune, delivering customised components for Automotive, Manufacturing, Food Industry and Defence Sector.

With over 8 years of experience, we are known for reliability, techincal accuracy and on-time execution.

Delivering precision components to some of the most respected names in the industries haved helped us innovate, evolve and reach new heights.

PRODUCTS
